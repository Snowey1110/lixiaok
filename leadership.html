Leadership

About

My name is Lixiao Kuang, and I am a graduating student studying at the University of Washington majoring in Applied Computing with leadership as my second discipline. I love teaching, unlocking new skills, and creating things using my computer science skills. In my free time, you can often find me lost in the enchanting melodies of the guqin, a traditional Chinese instrument, with an image of it on the right side.












Why

As a software engineer, I code because I have a passion for problem-solving and enjoy tackling challenging tasks. In the process of breaking down complex problems, designing solutions, and seeing my imagination coded into reality, it is like magic, and I am the wizard writing spellbooks! This dynamic and intellectually stimulating field allows me to continuously learn and grow, which keeps me motivated and engaged.

Core Values

Patience - In this fast-paced world, patience is often overlooked but crucial. As an educator and also as a student, patience means staying calm and analyzing the situation. It helps break down a problem, and helps out others, allowing me to listen, empathize, and create a supportive environment. By being patient, I achieve long-term goals and maintain a positive outlook.

Learning - In an ever-evolving world, the value of learning cannot be overstated. For me, it means constantly seeking knowledge and understanding that growth is a continuous process. Learning has empowered me to overcome obstacles and adapt to new situations, broadening my perspective and enhancing my skills. It also enriches my relationships by fostering open-mindedness and encouraging thoughtful conversations. By embracing learning, I stay curious, innovate, and achieve personal and professional fulfillment.

Service - In a society that often prioritizes individual success, the value of service is profound and transformative. For me, it means dedicating time and effort to help others, understanding that our collective well-being is intertwined. Service has allowed me to contribute positively to my community, building empathy and fostering a sense of purpose. It also strengthens my relationships, creating bonds through acts of kindness and mutual support. By committing to service, I find fulfillment in making a difference and cultivating a more compassionate world.

Leadership Identities

Silent Coder - As an introverted software engineer, I love to work in a group making contributions and perfecting the details without taking too much attention. It is a habit of mine to try to do things with the best efforts and bring the best possible outcome.

Patient Helper - Teaching has always been one of my greatest hobbies. As an educator working in a diverse environment with all races and genders, I try my best to help out the ones that have been struggling, since I have been like that when I first tried to learn English. Patience is one of my best behaviors that I can be proud of, not only does it help me analyze problems but I also never get anxious over stress.

Curious Explorer - Curiosity is the main factor that brought me to my path. I love to learn and absorb information, which leads me to the field of coding where I can explore the possibility of turning imagination into reality. The satisfaction of learning something new every day has kept me motivated to learn and always try out something new, whether it is problem-solving or learning a new skill.
